**<p style="text-align: center;">Suffer not the heretic.</p>**

Kushab is a dark world of greasy smoke and acrid incense, where the coppery scent of blood coats the tongue with every breath. In vaulted cathedrals of cyclopean architecture, the mortal races give worship to the First Ones and their kin—the demon-gods of Kushab who sit immortal at the side of Sotak, the First Across the Veil. But divinity on Kushab is not an abstract concept—through devotion, through the perfection of one's craft, and through sufficient blood, a mortal can ascend to godhood in their own right, joining the pantheon of Sotak's demon brood. It is this apotheosis that consumes the lives of all who worship under Kushab's dismal skies, the ultimate aim of the faithful and the devout.

But Kushab's society is experiencing schism as heresy takes hold. Freed from the prison-colony of Skand, the elven scientist Ukarth Ren took refuge on Kushab to recover his power, feasting on holy blood as his reclaimed planeswalker spark recuperated. Ren is no religious mystic, however, and has no interest in the trappings of faith—but Kushab is a realm rich in menfolk and ogres alike for him to practice his vivisection and flesh-change upon, eternally prying at the secrets of life. Many have flocked to his banner, eager to learn the dark arts of biology and chemistry under the tutelage of the apostate of Kushab, setting the scene for a schism that could challenge even the gods.

**The Apostate of Kushab** is a draftable premier set using Play Boosters, containing 266 cards (20 mythic rares, 60 rares, 100 uncommons, 81 commons, and 5 basic lands). ###Mechanics

<img align="left" width="200" height="353" style="margin:15px" src="https://i.ibb.co/tMMGPYXD/booster.png "APS booster"/>**The Apostate of Kushab** features the following mechanics:

**Communion:** Not all can achieve true immortality through divinity, but faith persists forever. Creatures with communion can be cast from your graveyard for their communion cost, returning to the battlefield as noncreature permanents with a finality counter on them. Every creature in **The Apostate of Kushab** with communion is an enchantment creature, which will mean their returned forms are noncreature enchantments.

**Oblation**: Offerings to Sotak are a sacred obligation. Oblation is a threshold ability which powers up your cards as long as you have either discarded a card or sacrificed a permanent this turn; this can be in the form of upgraded abilities or effects, or unlocking new activations or power and toughness boosts. Plan your offerings to maximise the benefits of faith.

**Evoke**: Returning from *Lorwyn*, evoke is an ability which allows the essence of a card to touch the battlefield without permanence. Casting a spell for its evoke cost will cause it to be sacrificed when it enters the battlefield, allowing you to avail of effects that trigger on it entering or leaving the battlefield. All cards in *The Apostate of Kushab* are also Saga enchantments, which means you have the choice of casting the Saga for its full effects over several turns or evoking it for its first chapter ability.

**Blood**: Returning from *Innistrad: Crimson Vow*, Blood tokens are a physical reminder of the power of Kushabi blood. Blood tokens are artifacts which allow you to discard a card and sacrifice the token to draw a new card, filtering away unneeded cards for a chance at new power. Bloods will also allow you to trigger your oblation abilities at will, and can discard a creature with communion in order to still avail of its abilities.
