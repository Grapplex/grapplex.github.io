**<p style="text-align: center;">A legacy generations in the making.</p>**

Once, two brothers fought over two precious gemstones. In a cave nestled amongst ancient ruins, in the dusty tombs of the Thran, Urza and Mishra split the mightstone and weakstone in their envy, and in doing so set in motion the doom of two worlds.

These stones had sealed an ancient portal between Dominaria and the machine hell of Phyrexia—with the gateway open once more, Yawgmoth's grand evolution could continue to influence Dominaria, starting with Mishra himself, and settling for no less than the entirety of the plane. In his guilt and his arrogance, Urza, the lord protector of Kroog, the defiler of Argoth, swore to deny the nine hells in their plan.

The chronicles of Urza's centuries-long struggle with Phyrexia would see the death of worlds, the lamentations of angels, and the manipulation of life and time—but would it be enough? Could anything prevent the inexorable evolution of the Ineffable himself? 

**Urza's Chronicle** is a draftable premier set using Play Boosters, containing 269 cards (20 mythic rares, 60 rares, 100 uncommons, 81 commons, 5 basic lands, and 3 basic-rarity Urza's lands). Bundle purchasers will also obtain a promotional alternate art copy of Karn, Legacy’s Steward, while all boosters have a chance of containing the Headliner legacy foil version of From Void Evolved Phyrexia. Boosters also have a chance of containing a Booster Fun old frame card, either a Saga or a basic land.

###Mechanics

<img align="left" width="200" height="353" style="margin:15px" src="https://i.ibb.co/Xq320DB/booster.png "URZ booster"/>**Urza's Chronicle** features the following mechanics:

**Historic:** Returning from *Dominaria*, the historic batch represents the breadth of history spanned by Urza's story, combining artifacts, legendary cards, and Sagas. As a batch, historic merely collates several kinds of card together in order to care about them on a card-by-card basis, with different effects possible when focusing on the history of Urza's chronicles.

**Recollection:** Memory is a funny thing, and Urza's is as long as it is troubled. Recollection aims to update the echo mechanic through a modern lens, allowing 'echo' payments to be made as an optional choice—a spell with recollection can be cast normally, or for its recollection cost. If cast for the latter, that recollection cost needs to be paid again at the beginning of your next main phase or the permanent is sacrificed. Do you want to split payment over two turns, or can you get your advantages from the card without needing to pay the second half?

**Farcycling:** An update to the original cycling mechanic, farcycling allows a deeper insight into your library in exchange for discarding the card—pay a card's farcycling cost and discard it in order to look at the top two cards of your library. You'll put one of those cards into your hand and the other in your graveyard—can you take advantage of the card selection and the graveyard filling?

**Sagas:** Part and parcel of the historic batch, Sagas also return from *Dominaria*. An enchantment subtype, Sagas trigger a new chapter ability each turn as they recount a story from the past of Dominaria, Serra's Realm, Phyrexia, Rath, or beyond.
