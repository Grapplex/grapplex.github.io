#Block Planning

---

Shortly after the introduction of Play Boosters and the associated restricting of premier sets to accommodate them, I decided to follow suit with an overall restructuring of my approach to set design. Inspired by the canon example of *Zendikar Rising*, *Kaldheim*, and *Strixhaven: School of Mages*, which had been loosely tied together with the theme of modal double-faced cards. Similarly, Grappleverse blocks are held together with broad mechanical ties, rather than narrative ones, and sets in a block are self-contained despite the presence of a mechanic throughline. Some ties are fairly blunt and direct, others may be slightly more high-concept, but all should be seen as just an additional element of set-dressing to allow contemporary sets to feel like they exist as part of a larger ecosystem.
